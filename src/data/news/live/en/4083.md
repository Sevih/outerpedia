**2\. New Side Story Added and Existing Side Stories Updated**

– Fortuna’s Side Story will be added.  
\> Players will be able to access it via the category, ‘New Encounters.’  
\> The Side Story will consist of 9 battle stages and 4 story stages.

**3\. \[Dimensional Crossroads\] Recruitment Event**

![](/images/news/live/en/4083-723a0a31.webp)

– The \[Dimensional Crossroads\] event will take place, allowing you to select and recruit one of several limited companions.

– Each recruitment grants 1 Limitied Mileage.

**8\. New Guild Raid Season** 

![](/images/news/live/en/4083-ea62826b.webp)

\-  The new Guild Raid Season “The Frost Legion” will begin according to the following schedule:   
\> Schedule: 11/19/2025 (Wed) 00:00 – 11/26/2025 (Wed) 00:00 UTC  
\> Calculation Period: 11/26/2025 (Wed) 00:00 – 12/03/2025 (Wed) 00:00 UTC  
\> Break Schedule: 12/03/2025 (Wed) 00:00 UTC – the start of the next season

■ How to Participate

1) Battles will be conducted across two phases against Geas Guardians and the main boss.  
– Phase 1: Consists of 5 stages where you must defeat 2 Geas Guardians in each stage.  
– Phase 2: Collaborate with guild members to defeat the main boss.  
– The main boss shares its Health with guild members, similar to the Geas Guardians.  
– Clearing Phase 1 progresses the battle to Phase 2.  
– If Phase 1 is not cleared within 2 days of the Guild Raid’s start, it automatically transitions to Phase 2 regardless of completion status.

■ Special Entry Tickets

2) If a boss is defeated when its health is below a certain threshold, you can obtain a Special Entry Ticket.  
– Special Entry Tickets allow you to challenge the previous boss using the remaining turns  
– For example, if you clear a boss with 3 turns remaining after consuming a Guild Raid Ticket, you can use a Special Entry Ticket to proceed with those 3 turns.  
\> Heroes used when earning a Special Entry Ticket are not marked as unavailable.  
\> If both a Special Entry Ticket and a Guild Raid Ticket are available, the Special Entry Ticket is consumed first.  
\> Special Entry Tickets expire at the daily reset time if unused.
