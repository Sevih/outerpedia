**4\. \[Guild Raid\]** **New Season Begins**

![](/images/news/live/en/5652-20c35615.webp)

A new season of Guild Raid, \[Dignity of the Golden Kingdom\], will be held according to the schedule below.  
– Season Period: 01/13/26 (Tue) after the maintenance – 01/20/26 (Tue) 00:00 UTC  
– Settlement Period: 01/20/26 (Tue) 00:00 UTC – 01/27/26 (Tue) 00:00 UTC  
– Reward Distribution Period: 01/27/26 (Tue) 00:00 UTC – 02/03/26 (Tue) 00:00 UTC  
– Off-Season Period: 02/03/26 (Tue) 00:00 UTC – until the start of the next season

**■ Battle Format**  
Battles are conducted across two phases, featuring battles against Geas Guardians and the main boss.  
  
Phase 1: Consists of 5 stages.  
– In each stage, two Geas Guardians will appear and must be defeated.

Phase 2: Guild members work together to defeat the main boss.  
– The main boss shares its HP across all guild members, similar to the Gias Guardians.  
– Phase 2 will begin once Phase 1 has been cleared.  
– If Phase 1 has not been cleared within 2 days after the Alliance Battle begins, Phase 2 will automatically start regardless of Phase 1 completion.

**■ Special Entry Tickets**  
– If a boss is defeated while its Health is below a certain threshold, you can obtain a Special Entry Ticket.  
– A Special Entry Ticket allows you to challenge the next boss using the remaining turns from the previous boss battle.  
  
Example:  
If you defeat a boss while its HP is below the required threshold and have 3 turns remaining after consuming a Guild Raid Ticket, you will be able to play 3 turns when entering with a Special Entry Ticket.

– Heros used to obtain a Special Entry Ticket will not be marked as unavailable.  
– If you have both Special Entry Tickets and Guild Raid Tickets, the Special Entry Ticket will be consumed first.  
– Unused Special Entry Tickets will be removed at the daily reset.
