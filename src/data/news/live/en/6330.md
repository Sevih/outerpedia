**2) Changes to \[Accuracy\] / \[Evasion\] / \[Heal on Hit Taken\]**  
– **From Stats and Gear Options, \[Accuracy\], \[Evasion\], and \[Heal on Hit Taken\] will be removed.**  
– **In Gear sub-stats, \[Damage Increase\] and \[Damage Reduction\] will be added.**

**(1) Allies & Monsters**  
– **3★ Kuro: In \[Soulslayer Role-Play!\], \[Buff Reversal\] will no longer include \[Accuracy\] or \[Evasion\].**  
– **All monsters’ \[Evasion\] will be set to 0.**  
– **Skill effects for monsters that include \[Accuracy\]/\[Evasion\] mechanics will be updated, including Dek’Ril/Mek’Ril from \[Special Request: Identification\] and monsters such as Regina.**  
– **Strategy/guide information for Dek’Ril/Mek’Ril and Regina will be updated to match the revised skill descriptions.**  
– **In Season 3 (Normal) 4-13, difficulty will be adjusted so that Maxei/Roxie (who appear with Hilde) will no longer use their Ultimate Skill.**

**(2) Gear**   
– **For accessories where the main stat is \[Accuracy\] (Fixed) or \[Evasion\] (Fixed) and the sub-stats include \[Accuracy\]% or \[Evasion\]%, those sub-stats will be converted first into \[Damage Increase\]% / \[Damage Reduction\]%.**   
– **If, at the time of conversion, \[Damage Increase\]% / \[Damage Reduction\]% already exists in the main stat or sub-stats, it will be replaced with a random sub-stat that does not duplicate the main stat or other sub-stats.**
