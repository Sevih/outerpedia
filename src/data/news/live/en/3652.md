Dear Masters,

**\[Compensation Details\]**  
– To address this issue, the following compensation will be provided:

**\[Compensation\]**  
– All Masters: Reward Board Points ×4,200

**\[Schedule\]**  
– Nov 5 (Thu) 08:30 UTC

**\[Claim Period\]**  
– Nov 5 (Wed) 08:30 UTC ~ Nov 10 (Mon) 23:59 UTC  
– Mail storage period: 7 days

* * *

We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this issue may have caused.

We will continue to do our best to provide a more stable gameplay environment.

Thank you.
