Hello Masters,

We would like to inform you about several issues that have been identified. Our apologies for the trouble while we work on fixing these issues.  
  
1\. An issue where the Pieces Reward pop-up may not appear starting from Day 2 of the Demiurge Contract check-in when the New Player Check-in is active.  
→ Rewards are still granted normally even if the pop-up doesn’t show.  
  
2\. An issue where the red dot may remain on the Mirsha Settlement Support icon after completing a Demiurge Contract Daily Mission and returning to the Lobby.  
→ We’re currently looking into this issue.  
  
3\. An issue where the title of the Shard Purchase pop-up in the Demiurge Contract screen is displayed as “Demiurge Luna’s Pieces” regardless of the selected Hero.  
→ This is a display issue only and does not affect the actual items obtained. You can still receive the selected hero’s pieces normally.  
  
Once again we apologize for any inconvenience caused.  
We will continue to inform our Masters regarding this issue via this notice.

Thank you.
