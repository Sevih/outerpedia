**2\. \[Core Fusion\] Hero Added – \[Snow\]**

![](/images/news/live/en/6615-726e10f4.webp)

(1) Core Fusion Requirements  
– Clear Season 2 (Normal) 10-12 \[The Conqueror and the Destroyer\] and reach 5★ Transcendence with the base Hero \[Snow\].  
– Tap the \[Core Fusion\] button located above the \[Hero\] button in the Lobby to access the Core Fusion screen.  
– A certain number of Fusion Cores are required to perform Core Fusion and to enhance the fused Hero.  
– Fusion Cores can be obtained as final boss clear rewards for each route in the \[Monad Gate\] content.

(2) Skills and Exclusive Equipment  
– Heros who undergo Core Fusion will have changes to the effects and animations of some skills, and a new Fusion Passive effect will be added.  
– Upon performing Core Fusion, exclusive Core Fusion equipment will be added.  
– Even after Core Fusion, you can continue to use the base Hero’s existing exclusive equipment.  
– Once Core Fusion is completed, enhancement values cannot be reset using the Time Rewinder.  
– When Core Fusion is performed, the base Hero’s level, evolution, transcendence, and affinity stages will be retained.  
– Once Core Fusion is completed, the original (pre-fusion) Hero can no longer be used.  
– When Core Fusion is performed, any Skill Manuals and Gold previously used for skill enhancement on the base Hero will be refunded.

**7\. Elemental Tower Expansion: Light/Dark**

![](/images/news/live/en/6615-351f198a.webp)

– The Tower of Light and Tower of Dark will be opened, each available up to 100 floors.  
  
1) Entry  
– Lobby > Adventure > Challenge > Elemental Tower

2) Entry Condition  
– Players can enter after clearing Skyward Tower 15F.  
– Players can enter 10 times a day.  
– There are no separate counts for each element. There are a total of 5 entry counts to enter any of the Elemental Towers.  
– The entry count will be decreased upon clearing the dungeon, but will not be decreased when failed or given up.

3) Hero Restrictions  
– In Elemental Tower, only the Heroes of the element of each tower can enter.

4) Debuff  
– A debuff is inflicted on the monsters in Elemental Tower based on the day of the week.  
– The Elemental Tower inflicted with debuffs changes based on the day of the week,  
– and the applied debuff is the same regardless of the element of the monsters.  
– If the day changes during the battle, the applied buffs during that battle will not change.

5) Reward  
– In the Tower of Light and Tower of Dark, you can obtain rewards such as Gold, Special Recruitment Ticket (Event), and Transistone  
– These are one-time rewards, and your progress will not be reset after clearing.
