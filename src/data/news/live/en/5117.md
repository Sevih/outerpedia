**4\. \[New Guild Raid\] –**  **New Season**

![](/images/news/live/en/5117-30f6c74a.webp)

– Event Period: December 16, 2025 After maintenance ~ December 23, 2025, 00:00 UTC  
– Settlement Period: December 23, 2025, 00:00 UTC ~ December 30, 2025, 00:00 UTC  
– Reward Period: December 30, 2025, 00:00 UTC ~ January 6, 2026, 00:00 UTC  
– Off-season Period: January 6, 2026, 00:00 UTC ~ Until the start of the next season

**■ Battle Structure**

1) Battles will be conducted across two phases against Geas Guardians and the main boss.  
– Phase 1: Consists of 5 stages where you must defeat 2 Geas Guardians in each stage.  
– Phase 2: Collaborate with guild members to defeat the main boss.  
– The main boss shares its Health with guild members, similar to the Geas Guardians.  
– Clearing Phase 1 progresses the battle to Phase 2.  
– If Phase 1 is not cleared within 2 days of the Guild Raid’s start, it automatically transitions to Phase 2 regardless of completion status.

■ Special Entry Tickets  
– If a boss is defeated when its health is below a certain threshold, you can obtain a Special Entry Ticket.  
– Special Entry Tickets allow you to challenge the previous boss using the remaining turns.  
– e.g. If you clear a boss with 3 turns remaining after consuming a Guild Raid Ticket, you can use a Special Entry Ticket to proceed with those 3 turns.  
– Heroes used when earning a Special Entry Ticket are not marked as unavailable.  
– If both a Special Entry Ticket and a Guild Raid Ticket are available, the Special Entry Ticket is consumed first.  
– Special Entry Tickets expire at the daily reset time if unused.
