**6\. Gear Preset Added**

\- Added a Gear Preset system which allows players to save up to 8 pieces of gear as a set and enables them to change and equip at once.

: Exclusive Equipment, an exclusive gear specialized for a hero, cannot be changed when switching the preset.

\- The preset button is available in the Hero Info pop-up and the Manage Gear screen.

: Hero > Gear > Change Gear > Preset

\- Players can save up to 20 presets per account.

\- The preset name can be up to 12 characters long.

\- Players can save frequently used presets as Favorites and set up to 5 preset Favorites.

: Favorites will be sorted to the top of the presets regardless of the preset settings and players can change the top/bottom order.

**7\. Guild Leader Authority Auto Transferring Added**

\- The Guild Leader authority will be delegated to the Guild Officer with the highest Contribution in case the Guild Leader remains inactive for 7 consecutive days.

\- The Guild Leader authority will be delegated to the Guild member with the highest Contribution, provided that they have logged on within the last 7 days, in the event that the Guild Officer has also been inactive for 7 consecutive days.

\- A pop-up message will be displayed upon logging in when the player assigned as a Guild Leader enters the Guild Base.

※ Due to the addition of the automatic Guild Leader authority transfer, the temporary policy of transferring the Guild Leader authority will end.

**8\. Guild Raid Participation Requirement Improvements**

\- Improvements will be made to the Guild Raid requirements so that Guild Members of the Guilds created during the Guild Raid period are also able to participate in the Guild Raid.

\- In exchange for relaxing the participation requirement, there will be a penalty when receiving the Guild rewards.

: The amount of Guild Rank rewards will be halved for members who join a new Guild during the Guild Raid period.

: You can claim Accumulated Guild Point Rewards and Geas Guardian Defeat Reward cleared before you joined the Guild.

: However, the rewards claimed in the previous Guild cannot be claimed again.

: After joining a new Guild, you can claim Accumulated Guild Point Reward after entering a battle once and the Geas Guardian Defeat Reward can be claimed regardless of whether you entered a battle or not.

\- Added a function to set Geas setting by team.

**9\. Guild Raid Season 3, Planet Control Unit - Epsilon**

**1) Participation**

\- Any player who is a member of a Guild may participate.

**2) Schedule**

\- The Guild Raid will follow a 4-week cycle, consisting of 10 days for the boss raid, 1 week to process results, and a 1-week break.

 : Schedule: 8/4 00:00 ~ 8/14 00:00 UTC

 : Calculation Period: 8/14 00:00 ~ 8/21 00:00 UTC

 : Break Schedule: 8/21 00:00 ~ before the next season begins

**3) How to Play**

\- To access the Guild Raid, simply tap on the Guild Board located in the Guild Base.

\- Upon entering the Guild Base for the first time while Guild Raid is active, a brief tutorial will commence.

\- The Guild Raid will consist of 1 main boss and 2 Geas Guardians.

 : Select the boss you wish to battle and form a team before proceeding to battle.

 : You can engage in battles with the main boss even if you haven't defeated the Geas Guardians.

 : You may also engage in practice battles with each boss.

\- Heroes who have participated in battle will become unable to participate again for the rest of the day and will be marked as unavailable.

\- Guild members who have taken part in the battle will receive participation rewards regardless of whether the boss was defeated or not.

\- All guild members will receive rewards when defeating a Geas Guardian.

※ From now on, the Guild Raid Boss will rotate in order from Season 1 to Season 3.

 : Maxwell > Ksai > Epsilon > Maxwell
